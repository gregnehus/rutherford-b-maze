An autonomous maze-solving robot of presidential quality

Authors:
 * Greg Nehus
 * Matt Odille


Overview:
 * Developed on a Lego Mindstorms NXT robot running ROBOTC firmware.
 * Completed in fulfillment of an assignment for the _Programming and System Design Using a Mobile Robot_ course at the University of Pittsburgh


Assumptions:
 * Grid-style maze with all 90-degree turns
 * Islands may be present
 * No knowledge of maze walls prior to execution
 * Given (x,y) coordinates of destination cell in advance


Goal:
 * Find a path to the destination cell, then travel back and forth between the destination and starting point as many times as possible in a specified time span
